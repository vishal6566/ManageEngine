A comprehensive identity management solution for Active Directory (AD) and cloud applications

AD360 enables you to securely manage users' identities, control access to critical resources, audit changes, empower users with self-service, and more. You can integrate AD360's different components together based on your specific requirements.

AD and Exchange Server management and reporting

This component of AD360 allows you to manage and get reports on AD, Exchange Server, Office 365, G Suite, and Skype for Business from a single console. It also features automation, workflow, and role-based delegation to minimize the administrative burden on IT admins and help desk technicians

  • One-stop user provisioning
  • AD objects management
  • NTFS and share permissions management
  • Automation and workflow for AD management
  • Reports on AD, Exchange, Office 365, and G Suite

    Real-time change auditing and alerting for on-premises AD and Azure AD

    This component of AD360 provides real-time audit reports and alerts on critical changes made to your on-premises AD and Azure AD environments. This component also helps you detect insider threats, analyze lockouts, and comply with IT regulatory mandates.

  • Real-time AD auditing
  • Logon activity monitoring
  • User behavior analytics
  • Privileged groups auditing
  • Preconfigured reports for IT compliance laws

    • AD self-service password management, password sync, and single sign-on

    By integrating AD360 with ADSelfService Plus, you gain support for AD self-service password management and single sign-on (SSO) for enterprise applications. It also helps secure your environment with endpoint multi-factor authentication and stronger password policies. ADSelfService Plus will reduce IT help desk tickets and rid end users' frustration of remembering too many passwords.

  • Self-service password reset and account unlock
  • Password expiration notification
  • Password sync
  • MFA-protected SSO for enterprise applications
  • Endpoint MFA for Windows, macOS, and Linux logons
  • Password Policy Enhance

    • Reporting, auditing, and monitoring for Exchange and Skype for Business Server

    This component of AD360 provides all the information you need to optimize and secure your organization’s Exchangewhether it’s on-premises or in the cloud. And it's not just for Exchange—you can get granular reports on activities happening in Skype for Business servers as well.

  • Reports on mailbox size, content, traffic, and more
  • OWA, ActiveSync, public folder, and distribution lists reports
  • Exchange auditing for logon audit and permission changes
  • Exchange services, DAG, storage, and database monitoring
  • Skype for Business reports

    • Manage, Monitor, Audit, and Report on Office 365

    AD360 provides exhaustive, preconfigured reports on Office 365 to help you perform complex tasks including bulk user management, bulk mailbox management, secure delegation, and more. AD360 monitors Office 365 services around the clock and sends you instant email notifications about service outages. AD360 makes compliance management easy with built-in compliance reports and advanced auditing and alerting features to keep your Office 365 setup secure.     • AD, Exchange, and Office 365 backup and recovery

    Recovery Manager Plus, a component of AD360, takes care of all your AD, Exchange Server, and Office 365 backup and recovery needs. Back up AD objects, Exchange mailbox items, Office 365 mailboxes, folders, sites, etc., and restore them to any previous version in a few clicks.

  • AD object backup and restoration
  • Exchange mailbox backup and restoration
  • Office 365 mailbox, folder, site, etc., backup and restoration
  • Incremental backups and backup retention
  • Attribute and item-level restoration
  • AD rollback
